Our History

Founded in Guwahati, Serving the North East

Established in 2017, RSL Enterprise emerged with a single core objective: to bring structural financial expertise and inclusion to businesses and individuals across North East India.

We recognized that small entrepreneurs, middle-income groups, and local shops in Assam faced significant barriers when attempting to prepare bank-standard loan documentation, register for government subsidies, or file taxes. RSL Enterprise fills this gap by acting as a highly efficient bridge—offering professional-grade credit appraisal reports and CA-certified document services at a fraction of standard consulting costs.

Our Mission

To promote grassroots financial inclusion by rendering high-quality documentation accessible and hassle-free.

Our Commitment

Honest advisory, prompt SLA timelines, and reports formatted strictly to banking standards.

RSL DNA

Terms of Service

Welcome to RSL Enterprise. By accessing our services, you agree to comply with and be bound by the following terms:

  • Service Scope: RSL Enterprise provides financial project reporting, CA certifications, and business registrations based entirely on inputs provided by the client.
  • Client Responsibility: Clients must verify the accuracy of all submitted data. RSL Enterprise is not liable for loan rejections due to false information provided by clients.
  • Order Confirmation: Service orders are officially placed and scheduled only after successful verification of the client's payment.
  • Electronic Delivery: All final deliverables (DPRs, CMA spreadsheets, certificates) will be delivered as soft copies via email in PDF, DOCX, or XLSX formats.

Refund & Changes Policy

Our commitment is to deliver value. We maintain strict SLAs for service delivery and modifications:

  • Delivery SLA: Standard delivery of soft copies takes 3 to 5 working days from order placement.
  • Refund Window: Clients are eligible for a full refund within 4 working days of payment, in the unlikely event that RSL Enterprise delivers an incorrect report or fails to deliver within the stipulated SLA timeframe.
  • Report Revisions: Modifications to the project reports are acceptable post-delivery under the following terms:
    • No changes to the total requested loan amount are acceptable once documentation begins.
    • No modifications will be accepted after 2 working days of delivery of the project report.
    • A revision fee of ₹200 will be charged for any changes.
    • Revisions are acceptable only once per report.

Privacy & Cookies Policy

RSL Enterprise prioritizes the protection of your sensitive financial and personal data:

  • Data Collection: We collect only necessary personal identifiers (PAN, Aadhaar, Name) and financial summaries to fulfill requested tax returns and project reports.
  • Data Sharing: Client data is strictly confidential. We do not sell or lease client details to third-party marketing firms. Information is shared only with regulatory authorities as requested by the client (e.g. Income Tax Department, MSME portal).
  • Children's Privacy: RSL Enterprise does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under 13. If discovered, such records are promptly deleted.
  • Cookies: Our static portal uses minimal cookies solely to optimize page loading times and track navigation statistics to improve overall site experience.
