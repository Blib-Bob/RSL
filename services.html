What does a bank-ready DPR include?

Our Detailed Project Reports (DPR) cover exhaustive parameters required by Nationalized (SBI, PNB, UCO) and Private banks:

  • Project feasibility review & technical viability
  • 7-Year Projected Balance Sheets & Profitability calculations
  • Depreciation charts & Capital statements
  • DSCR (Debt Service Coverage Ratio) & ROCE computations
  • BEP (Break-Even Point) calculations
