A Detailed Project Report (DPR) is an exhaustive business model plan. It outlines the financial requirements, means of finance, capital allocations, and projected feasibility ratios (like ROCE, Debt Equity, and DSCR) for the next 7 years. Banks mandate this documentation to analyze the commercial viability of a project before approving term loans or composite credits.

We maintain a 99% track record in loan project report approvals. Because we structure the reports strictly according to regulatory banking guidelines and credit manager standards, banks can quickly verify the key risk factors (BEP, DSCR, TOL/TNW) and proceed with loans smoothly.

RSL Enterprise has a prompt Service Level Agreement (SLA). Soft copies of Detailed Project Reports (DPR) and CA Certificates are delivered straight to your email within 3 to 5 working days from payment confirmation. Flat rate filings (like MSME) are usually completed within 3 days.

Cash Credit (CC) limit is a working capital facility provided by banks for 1 year. To renew this limit, banks require a Credit Monitoring Arrangement (CMA) data sheet, which details past actual turnovers alongside projected financial allocations for 3 years into the future. We specialize in structuring CMA reports that satisfy bank criteria.

Udyam Registration is the official certificate provided by the Ministry of MSME, Govt of India. Holding an MSME certificate qualifies your enterprise for priority sector bank lending rates, credit guarantee schemes, concession on electricity bills, capital subsidies, and legal protection against delayed payments from buyers.
